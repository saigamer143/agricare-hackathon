Our Services

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to help farmers quickly and accurately identify crop diseases. By uploading a photo of the affected plant, farmers can receive immediate information on the disease and actionable remedies to treat it.

Rice Leaf Blast

It is a serious fungal disease caused by the pathogen Magnaporthe oryzae. It primarily affects rice plants, leading to significant yield losses.

Potato Late Blate

It is caused by a water mold that thrives in cool, moist conditions. It spreads rapidly through spores in the air and water, infecting both potato foliage and tubers.

Wheat Brown Rust

It is a fungal disease that affects wheat crops and can lead to significant yield losses if not managed effectively.

Rice Neck Blast

It is a serious disease affecting rice plants, caused by the fungus Magnaporthe oryzae, which is the same pathogen responsible for Rice Leaf Blast

Corn Gray Leaf Spot

It is a fungal disease caused by the pathogen Cercospora zeae-maydis, affecting corn (maize) plants and potentially leading to reduced yields.

Wheat Yellow Rust

It is a significant fungal disease that affects wheat crops, leading to substantial yield losses if not effectively managed.

About Us
